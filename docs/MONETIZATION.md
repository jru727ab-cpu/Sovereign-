# Monetization Plan — CIVILTAS

## Philosophy

CIVILTAS monetizes **knowledge and time** — not pay-to-win mechanics. The core idle loop is fully free. Premium content expands narrative depth and convenience.

---

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Secrets Packs (IAP — One-Time)

| Pack | Contents | Price |
|------|----------|-------|
| Gnosis Bundle I | 3 Lore secrets | $2.99 |
| Operative Dossier | 2 Survival Intel secrets | $1.99 |
| Complete Secrets Library | All 12 secrets | $6.99 |

**Rationale:** Secrets are narrative/cosmetic — they improve the Forecast Meter and reveal lore but don't gate core gameplay.

### 2. VIP Subscription (Monthly)

- **$4.99/month** — Compass Bearer tier
- 2x ore production multiplier
- Exclusive secrets (VIP-only lore)
- Ad-free experience
- Season pass included

### 3. Season Pass (Annual Event)

- **$9.99** — Catastrophe Cycle Season Pass
- Seasonal story content
- Exclusive rewards and cosmetics
- Participation in catastrophe prediction events

### 4. Remove Ads (One-Time)

- **$1.99** — Permanent ad removal
- Interstitial ads shown after every 3rd manual collect (non-VIP)

### 5. Ore Accelerator Packs (Future)

- Small ore bundles for players who want to speed up early progression
- Designed to be optional, not necessary

---

## Ethical Guardrails

- **No loot boxes** — All purchases are deterministic
- **No forced ads** — Ads are optional for bonus ore (rewarded) or can be removed permanently
- **No artificial timers** — Offline progress is generous (8 hours cap)
- **Children's privacy** — COPPA compliant; no behavioral targeting
- **Transparent pricing** — No dark patterns; prices shown clearly before purchase
