# CIVILTAS Monetization Plan

## Guiding Principles
1. **Fun first** — any friction introduced by monetization must be optional
2. **Transparent** — players always know what they're buying
3. **No hard paywalls** — 100% of core content is free
4. **Compliant** — Play Billing on Google Play; Stripe for direct APK; no unlicensed gambling mechanics

## Revenue Streams

### Season Pass (primary)
- Monthly or seasonal subscription
- Bonus daily tasks (+3 per day)
- Exclusive cosmetic building skins
- 1.5× Gnosis generation (cosmetic effect; no P2W)
- Price target: $4.99/month

### Resource Bundles (secondary)
- "Survivor Starter Pack" — one-time purchase for new players
- Small iron/copper/food bundles — convenience for stuck players
- Strict cap: no bundle is 10× better than free play
- Price range: $0.99 – $4.99

### Gnosis Shards (premium currency)
- Earned at 1 Gnosis/100hr free
- Purchasable in bundles
- Used only for: skip construction timers, cosmetic building upgrades
- NOT used for: story progression, competitive advantage

### Optional Ads
- Video ad → 2× mining rate for 30 minutes
- Maximum 3 ads per day per player
- Rewarded only; no interstitials or banners

### Cosmetics
- Building skin packs (Stone Age, Renaissance, Futurist, Secret Society)
- Civilization name plates and banners
- Character portrait frames

## What We Explicitly Avoid
- Random loot boxes (regulatory risk + player backlash)
- Energy systems that stop play
- Ads that interrupt gameplay
- Pay-to-win gear

## Distribution
- **Phase 1**: Free APK sites (ApkPure, APKMirror) — Stripe payments
- **Phase 2**: Google Play — Play Billing required for all IAP
- **Phase 3**: iOS App Store — StoreKit required
