## Building Multipliers (placeholder)
Each building level multiplies the mining rate for associated resources by 1.1×.

## Catastrophe Cycle Economy
- Normal phase: standard rates apply
- Alert phase (level 5+): Vault building becomes critical — resources not in vault are at risk
- Catastrophe: non-vaulted resources reduced by 50–80% based on preparation level
- Rebuild phase: first 24 hours post-catastrophe have 2× rates
